How to Use Nonogin Math Lessons

Welcome to Nonogin! Here’s how to use the math lessons:

  • Click on a grade button from the home page to open lessons & tests.
  • Each grade has 8 lessons and 5 tests in one page.
  • Each lesson contains multiple math problems. Enter your answers in the boxes.
  • Click "Check All Answers" to see your score for all lessons and tests.
  • Problems are randomized every time you open the page.
  • Grades 5–9 include algebra, fractions, decimals, percentages, and multi-step problems.
